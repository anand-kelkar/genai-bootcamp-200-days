## What is Inheritance?
It is an OOP concept where child class inherits property of parent class.This allows reuse.

## What is encapsulation?
Encasulation in OOP refer to the process of protecting the data and allowing controlled access to it through properties and mathods

## Why not make every variable public??
One of the OOP concept is encapsulation that leads to controlled access to data through private and protected identifiers and provide access to them with properties and methods. If we will make every variable public, it voilets the encapsulation principle and leads to security and  maintanance risk and variable state may become invalid
